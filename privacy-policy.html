Sudoku Solver Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2023

Personal Information:

Sudoku Solver does not collect any personal information from its users. The app is designed to be used without any form of registration or login, ensuring that no personal identifiers are captured when using the application.

Non-Personal Information:

We do not collect non-personal information like user device information, location, etc.

Usage Data:

We do not collect data relating to your use of the Sudoku Solver app, such as the puzzles you solve or the length of your usage sessions.

Cookies:

Sudoku Solver does not use cookies. Cookies are files with a small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored on your device's internal memory.

Third-Party Links:

Children's Privacy:

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.
